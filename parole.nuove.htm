A reminder

 
ARTICLES and ADJECTIVES must take the same gender
as the NOUN they are associated with.

UOMO:  UN UOMO  ITALIANO
 
DONNA:   UNA DONNA ITALIANA

UN  ("a") is the masculine form of the indefinite article

UNA ("a") is the feminine form of the indefinite article

ITALIANO - ITALIANA
are respectively the masculine and the feminine form of the adjective.
